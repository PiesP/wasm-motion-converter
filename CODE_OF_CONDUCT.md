# Code of Conduct

We want a friendly, inclusive, professional community.

## Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus on issues and technical content.
- No harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language.
- Do not share private or sensitive data.

## Scope

All repository spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and code review.

## Reporting

- Security/privacy concerns: use `.github/SECURITY.md`.
- Other concerns: open an issue asking for moderator attention (keep details minimal).

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation to keep the community safe and productive.
